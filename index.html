About

I am a researcher and engineer working at the intersection of trustworthy AI, behavioral learning dynamics, and reliability-aware autonomy. My work spans applied ML reliability evaluation and safety-centric design for autonomous aerial systems, along with real-world data reliability work.

I care about systems that are not only performant but also reliable, interpretable, and behaviorally stable.
