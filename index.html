About

My core interest is reliability in learning systems: when environments shift, uncertainty spikes, or feedback becomes noisy, models often become overconfident, brittle, or unstable.

I work on bridging trustworthiness (UQ + calibration) with autonomy (planning + control), using simulation-driven evaluation and reliability-aware design principles.

Quick Snapshot

  • Autonomy: UAV path planning, safety constraints

What I want to prove (research style)

1) Diagnose

Detect reliability failures early (drift, overconfidence, instability).

2) Control

Introduce feedback/guidance that stabilizes learning dynamics.

3) Validate

Show robustness using stress-tests and autonomy-relevant metrics.
