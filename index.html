About Me

I am a researcher and engineer working at the intersection of Trustworthy AI, behavioral learning dynamics, and reliability-aware autonomy. My work integrates applied machine learning, AI reliability evaluation, and safety-centric design for autonomous aerial systems. I am equally committed to community-driven education, having taught and mentored learners across rural, urban, and underserved regions.

I care about AI systems that are not only performant but also reliable, interpretable, and behaviorally stable. My research and outreach emphasize human-centered thinking, continuous learning, and responsible innovation.
