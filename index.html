About

My core interest is reliability in learning systems: when environments shift, uncertainty spikes, or feedback becomes noisy, models often become overconfident, brittle, or unstable.

I work on bridging trustworthiness (uncertainty + calibration) with autonomy (planning + control), using simulation-driven evaluation and reliability-aware design.
