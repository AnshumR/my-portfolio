About Me

I am a researcher and engineer working at the intersection of Trustworthy AI, Behavioral Meta-Learning, and Reliability-Aware Autonomous Systems. My work integrates applied machine learning, AI reliability, and safety-centric design for autonomous aerial systems. I am equally committed to community-driven education, having taught and mentored learners across rural, urban, and underserved regions.

I believe in AI systems that are not only performant but also reliable, interpretable, and behaviourally stable. My research and outreach both emphasize human-centered thinking, continuous learning, and responsible innovation.

Independent Research (2024–present)

  • How calibration and confidence drift during learning
