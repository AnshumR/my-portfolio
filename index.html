About Me

I work at the intersection of Trustworthy AI, learning behavior, and reliability-aware autonomy. My projects span research on reliability in learning systems (PGAR), UAV path planning, and applied reliability work in data/engineering settings.

I care about AI systems that are not only performant but also reliable, interpretable, and behaviourally stable—especially when uncertainty spikes or environments shift.
